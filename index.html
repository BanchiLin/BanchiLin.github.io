Selected Projects

JobMate: AI-Mediated Career Exploration through Peer Experiences

Oct 2025 – Apr 2026

JobMate is an interactive system that transforms authentic career-related posts from Xiaohongshu into persona-grounded conversational agents, helping young job seekers move from passive browsing to active career sensemaking.

Role: Project lead; system design; data collection and processing; prompt design; user study; qualitative analysis; writing.

Methods: LLM-based data processing, persona construction, self-determination theory, prototype design, user study.

Status: Paper, code, video, and web prototype available internally.

Stock Prediction with Emotion and Emoji Representations

Jul 2025 – Sep 2025

This project investigated whether emoji-based emotional representations can improve stock prediction performance on the StockEmotions dataset.

Role: Literature review; result analysis; paper writing.

Methods: LSTM, emotion representation, emoji encoding.

Outcome: Published paper. Code not publicly available.

Text-to-Cypher with LLMs and ReAct

Mar 2025 – Jun 2025

This project explored how large language models convert natural language questions into Cypher queries for graph databases.

Role: Literature review; review of existing approaches; experimental evaluation with LLMs and ReAct.

Methods: Large language models, ReAct framework, semantic parsing.

Finding: Initial results were limited, with accuracy around 20%.

Metaphorical Representation of Power Concepts

Dec 2022 – Feb 2023

This psychology experiment examined how power concepts are metaphorically associated with font size and color in a Chinese cultural context.

Role: Experimental program design; partial experiment execution; full data analysis; report writing.

Methods: Stroop paradigm, reaction time analysis.

Finding: Participants responded faster when high-power concepts appeared in large or golden fonts, and low-power concepts appeared in small or gray fonts.
